Big Dye Terminator Sequencing

Reagents:

  • Big Dye Terminator kit from Perkin Elmer

  • Gene specific primers (need 3.2 pmol/reaction)

  • Sample DNA:

    • 200-300 ng for dsDNA

    • 90-150 ng for PCR products

  • ddH2O

  • Isopropanol

  • Mineral Oil
