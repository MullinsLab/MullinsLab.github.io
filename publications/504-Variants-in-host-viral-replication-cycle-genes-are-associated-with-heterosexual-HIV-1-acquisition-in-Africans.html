Citation Information

Bigham AW, Mackelprang RD, Celum C, De Bruyn G, Beima-Sofie K, John-Stewart G, Ronald A, Mugo NR, Buckingham K, Bamshad MJ, Mullins JI, McElrath MJ, Lingappa JR (2014). Variants in host viral replication cycle genes are associated with heterosexual HIV-1 acquisition in Africans. Journal of acquired immune deficiency syndromes (1999), 66(2), 127-34. (pubmed) (doi)

Abstract

We evaluated genetic variants in 51 candidate genes encoding proteins that interact with HIV-1 during the virus life cycle for association with HIV-1 outcomes in an African cohort.
